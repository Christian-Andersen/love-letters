June 12, 1944

To my dearest — from the front
My love, The days have been long and the nights longer still. I tuck this paper into my coat and imagine your hand holding it. The fields smell of rain, and every distant rumble makes my heart lean toward you. I found a locket today with a photograph inside — a small thing that reminded me of the life I hope to return to. Keep a candle by the window for me, as you always do. Forever yours, E.
